📐 Simple Crystal Charging Formula

Universal rules governing atmospheric moonlight penetration & crystal capacity charge rates

1. Moonlight Strength
Moonlight Strength = Moon Light × Sky Height × Sky Clearness × City Lights Blockage
  • Moon Light: How full is the Moon (0% to 100%)
  • Sky Height: How high is it in the night sky (0.0 to 1.0)
  • Sky Clearness: Cloud block multiplier (100% - clouds)
  • City Lights: Dark sky rating multiplier (10 - City Lights Class)
2. Combined Charging Speed
Combined Charging Speed = (Total Sky Power / 100) × Crystal Receptiveness × 2.5 %/hour
  • Total Sky Power: Combined selected chargers (Sun, Moon, Venus, Jupiter, Mars)
  • Crystal Receptiveness: Unique mineral multiplier (e.g. Moonstone = 2.0x)
  • If Total Sky Power is 0, the crystal drains at a steady speed.
